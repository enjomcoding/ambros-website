About Ambros Cornik

A pioneer cornik maker from Bagar, Candon City.

Ambros Ilocos Cornik is the pioneer cornik manufacturer in Candon City established in 1980. The business continues to produce safe, quality cornik at affordable prices for customer satisfaction.

"We make customers, not a sale."

FDA Registered Manufacturer

Ambros Ilocos Cornik is located at Bagar, Candon City, Ilocos Sur and is FDA registered as a food manufacturer.

Seven Variant-Flavors

Garlic, cheese, barbeque, adobo-spicy, sweet & spicy, sweet, and adobo-garlic flavors.
